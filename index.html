Tanner's white noise music player for babies

Sometimes, calm a new baby and let the little one fall asleep could be difficult. However, white noise is definitely helpful. So, I'd love to create this simple music player.

#How to use# choice one music or white noise your baby love, and set a timer to turn off the music in case you forgot to turn it off before you and your little baby fall asleep.
